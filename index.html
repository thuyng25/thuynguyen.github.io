About me

I've been doing data work longer than my diploma suggests.

Before I ever opened a Jupyter notebook, I was forecasting product orders, tracking margins, and figuring out why a slow Tuesday looked different from a slow Thursday — all for my family's nail salon. That instinct for "what's actually going on in these numbers" is what pulled me into data visualization. Now I build dashboards and web apps that do the same thing at a bigger scale: turn a pile of numbers into something a person can actually use.
