About me

I've been doing data work longer than my diploma suggests.

I started with Excel in operations at a small business, tracking product orders, margins, and day-to-day demand patterns. That’s where I first learned to think in data and connect numbers to real business decisions.

At the University of Washington Bothell, I earned a degree in Data Visualization and a minor in Informatics. I built projects including a GIS mapping app on food access and transit equity in Seattle and a web app focused on campus safety.

I’m now looking to apply this mix of data analysis and operations experience in roles focused on data-driven decision-making and operational precision.
